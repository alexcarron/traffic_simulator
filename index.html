HOW TO PLAY

A
Move to the left lane
S
Move to the center lane
D
Move to the right lane

You are the blue car at the top of the screen and you need to get to the end while dodging the orange traffic cones in the way.

Using the keyboard controls, you'll dodge the traffic cones in your way so you can get to work before you run out of time. The timer at the top shows how long you have to get at work before your LATE!

You can see how close you are to work using the progress bar on the right and the percentage. You'll also see a line with "WORK" labeled on it when your close to the end. If you hit a traffic clone you'll be pushed back even farther from work.

If you reach work before the time runs out you win, but if the timer runs out before then, you'll lose. Have fun!
